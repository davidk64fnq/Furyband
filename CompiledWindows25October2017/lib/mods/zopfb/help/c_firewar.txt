#####R=== Fire Warriors ===

#####GDescription

This War Mage uses fire to supplement his sword skills.  At level 5, he
becomes resistant to fire.  At level 25 he is surrounded by a fiery
sheath, and at level 45 he becomes completely immune to fire.
